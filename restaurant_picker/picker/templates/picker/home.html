What is Where will we Eat today?

Where will we Eat today? is a web app that helps you decide where to eat. It is a simple app that will randomly choose a restaurant for you to eat at.

Feeling Decisive?

To see a list of all the restaurants to visit just click the 'restaurants' link in the nav.

Don't want to make a decision?

navigate to `Where to Eat?` and click the button for us to choose for you!
